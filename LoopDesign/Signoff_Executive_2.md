## 3. The Reconstruction Verdict
Does the documentation pass the **Reconstruction Threshold**? 
**Yes, but it will be highly inefficient.** 

The raw data is 100% present. We have the data models, the system prompts, the architectural pattern (Dependency Injection & Event Bus), and file-by-file breakdown. No information is fundamentally "lost." However, the lack of an `18_Reconstruction_Guide.md` and synthesized dependency maps means the reconstruction effort will require a heavy initial analysis phase to piece the puzzle back together.
